BeanieBots is one of three teams at Heritage Academy Gateway. Our team consists of 14 competitive teams members, and 5 non competitive team members. Together we have progressed through the year, creating our little bot we named bean and growing together as a team. Sam Webster is our team leader, and Spencer is the second in command. We faced many challenges, and also gained many rewards. In our first competition, We went into the semi finals. Although we didnt win, with us being a rookie team that's pretty good. At the competitions, we try to have everyone doing something, because everyone in the team is important. Throughout this past year, we have grown our team's working abilities, and our knowledge. Even though it's not always easy work, we will always remember our robotics expirence.
