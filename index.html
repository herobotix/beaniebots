BeanieBots is one of three teams at Heritage Academy Gateway. Our team consists of 14 competitive teams members, and 5 non competitive team members. Sam Webster is our team leader, and Spencer is the second in command. Together we have progressed through the year, creating our little bean (we named our bot bean) and growing together as a team. We faced many challenges, and also many rewards. In our first competition, we went into the semi finals, we didnt win, but as a rookie team we say that is pretty good. At the competitions, we try to continue having everyone be doing something, because everyone is a part of our team. Throughout this past year, we have grown our team working abilities, and our knowledge. Even though BeanieBots may not be fully together, we will all remember our robotics expirence.
